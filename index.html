02 Who We Are

Execution of Projects with Excellence and Customer Satisfaction through Innovative Thinking

Established in 2021 at Pune, Maharashtra, Lesca Tech is registered under MSME. We provide complete facilities for Fabrication of Cast Steel, Stainless Steel, and Alloy Steel equipment. Our mission is to apply new project techniques with improved tools continuously to deliver projects on time, utilizing sound Project Management principles in our day-to-day activities.

Providing an entrepreneurial environment with a passion for Project Management, we have developed a global network of expertise. We offer competitive prices with ecofriendly technologies like PPH, PPS, and FRP, ensuring excellence with customer satisfaction.
